## Browsing Games

### 🔍 Finding Games

#### Method 1: Category Filtering
1. Scroll to **"Explore by Category"** section
2. Click category buttons: Action, RPG, Puzzle, Strategy, Sports, Adventure, Simulation, Casual, Indie, Multiplayer, Horror
3. Games grid updates automatically
4. Active category highlighted in orange

#### Method 2: Featured Games Table
1. Scroll to **"⭐ Featured Games"** section
2. View top-rated games in table format
3. See: Title, Category, Rating, Price, Download Count
4. Click **"Download"** button directly from table

#### Method 3: Games Grid
1. Scroll to **"All Games"** section
2. Browse beautiful game cards
3. Each card shows:
   - Game cover image
   - Title and category
   - Short description (first 100 chars)
   - Rating and download count
   - Price
   - Download and Reviews buttons

### 🎴 Game Card Information
- **Title**: Name of the game
- **Category Badge**: Orange badge showing category
- **Description**: First 100 characters with "..."
- **Rating**: Star rating with download count
- **Price**: $0 for free, or price for paid games
- **Download Button**: ⬇️ to download/purchase
- **Reviews Button**: 💬 to see comments and ratings

## Creating Your Account

### 📝 Sign Up

1. Click **"Sign Up"** button in navigation bar
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Full Name**: Your name
   - **Email**: Valid email address
   - **Password**: At least 6 characters
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. You'll be automatically logged in
5. Success notification appears ✅

### ✅ Sign Up Requirements
- Email must contain "@"
- Password minimum 6 characters
- Each email can only be registered once
- Name required

### ❌ Common Sign Up Issues
- "User with this email already exists" → Use different email
- "Please fill in all fields" → Complete all fields
- "Password must be at least 6 characters" → Use longer password
- "Please enter a valid email" → Include "@" in email

## Logging In

### 🔐 Login

1. Not logged in? Click **"Login"** button in navigation

## Downloading Games

### 📥 Free Games

1. Browse games and find a free one ($0 price)
2. Click **"⬇️ Download"** button
3. **Must be logged in** - if not, you'll be prompted to login
4. Download completes immediately ✅
5. Game appears in your Dashboard → My Downloads
6. Download count increases

### 💳 Paid Games

1. Browse games and click **"⬇️ Download"** on paid game
2. **Payment Modal** appears showing:
   - Game title
   - Category
   - Total price
3. Choose payment method (see next section)
4. Enter payment details
5. Click **"Complete Payment"**
6. After successful payment, game downloads
7. Game added to your Download history

### ⚠️ Download Requirements
- Must be logged in (creates user account first)
- Paid games require valid payment information
- Free games download instantly (after login)
- Downloads persist in your profile

## Payments

### 💰 Payment Methods

#### Method 1: Bank/Card Payment
1. In payment modal, stay on **"💳 Card/Bank"** tab
2. Fill in card details:
   - **Cardholder Name**: Name on card
   - **Card Number**: 16 digits (e.g., 1234 5678 9012 3456)
   - **Expiry Date**: MM/YY format
   - **CVV**: 3-4 digit security code
3. All fields required
4. Click **"Complete Payment"**

**Validation Rules**:
- Card number: Exactly 16 digits
- Expiry: MM/YY format
- CVV: 3-4 digits
- All fields required

#### Method 2: Mobile Money Payment
1. In payment modal, click **"📱 Mobile Money"** tab
2. Fill in details:
   - **Full Name**: Your name
   - **Phone Number**: Your mobile number
   - **Mobile Provider**: Select from dropdown
     - Airtel Money
     - Vodafone Cash
     - MTN Money
     - Globus Money
     - Other
3. All fields required
4. Click **"Complete Payment"**

**Validation Rules**:
- Phone number: At least 10 digits
- Provider: Must be selected
- Name required

### 🔒 Payment Security Note
- This is a demo application
- Payments are simulated (no real charges)
- Don't use real card numbers
- Always use real payment systems in production

### ✅ Payment Success
- Green success notification appears
- Game added to downloads
- Notification: "✅ [Game Name] downloaded successfully!"
- Game appears in Dashboard

## Comments & Reviews

### ⭐ Leaving a Review

1. On any game card, click **"💬 Reviews"** button
2. **Comments Modal** opens showing:
   - Game title
   - All existing reviews
   - Comment form at bottom

3. Rate the game:
   - Click stars (1-5) in "Rate this game" section
   - Selected rating highlights in orange
   - Shows "X/5" at the top

4. Write your comment:
   - Type in text area: "Share your thoughts..."
   - Maximum no limit, be detailed

5. Click **"Post Comment"** button
6. Success notification appears ✅
7. Your comment appears in the list

### ❌ Review Requirements
- Must be logged in
- Must select rating (1-5 stars)
- Must write some text

### 📋 Viewing Reviews

1. Click **"💬 Reviews"** on any game
2. See all reviews posted:
   - Commenter name
   - Star rating (⭐ X/5)
   - Comment text
   - Date posted
3. Reviews displayed in order (newest first)

### 🗑️ Your Comments History
1. Go to Dashboard: Click **"Dashboard"** button
2. Click **"My Comments"** tab
3. See all your submitted reviews
4. Shows: Game title, rating, comment text, date

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In
- ❌ "Invalid email or password" → Check spelling of email and password
- ❌ Forgot password? → No recovery in demo (create new account)
- ✅ Ensure caps lock is off

### Can't Download Games
- ❌ "Please log in to download games" → Click Login first
- ❌ Paid game needs payment → Complete payment form
- ✅ Free games download instantly after login

### Comments Not Showing
- ❌ Not logged in → Log in first before posting
- ❌ Empty comment text → Write something in text area
- ❌ No rating selected → Click stars to rate (1-5)
- ✅ Refresh page if comment doesn't appear immediately

### Data Not Saving
- ❌ Clearing browser cache → Deletes all stored data
- ❌ Using private/incognito mode → Data doesn't persist
- ✅ Use normal browsing mode for persistent storage

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A**: Data stored locally on your device. No external servers. Clear your browser cache to delete data. This is a demo application.

### Q: Can I download on different devices?
**A**: No, data is per-device/browser. You need separate accounts on different devices.

### Q: How do I change subscription?
**A**: Click "Subscription" → Select new tier. Takes effect immediately.

### Q: Can I delete my account?
**A**: In this demo, clear your browser cache or create new account. Production version would have proper account deletion.

### Q: Do I keep my downloads if I change subscription?
**A**: Yes, downloads history persists. Changing subscription doesn't affect downloads.

### Q: Why can't I use real payments?
**A**: This is a demo. For production, integrate real payment gateway (Stripe, PayPal, etc.).
